# 📺 Civic Nightmare: The Bureaucracy RPG

**Civic Nightmare** is a satirical, top-down RPG built in **Godot 4.3** that explores the absurdity of modern global governance, corporate dominance, and the digital age. You play as a scrawny, debt-burdened citizen on a desperate quest to renew a passport, navigating a world where reality is filtered through 16-bit aesthetics and 90s television glitches.

## 🕹️ Game Features

### 🏛️ The Great Signature Quest
Navigate a procedurally detailed overworld and interact with the "Architects of the Spectacle." To get your document signed, you must confront the egos of:
- **Donald Trump**: In his Golden Eagle monument.
- **Elon Musk**: Inside a minimalist SpaceShip HQ.
- **Ursula von der Leyen**: Guarding the Labyrinth of Regulations.
- **Vladimir Putin**: Within a cross-shaped bunker.
- **Christine Lagarde**: Managed by interest rates and invoices.
- **Emmanuel Macron**: Offering philosophy lectures instead of help.
- **Xi Jinping**: Controlling the supply lines of reality.

### 🛸 Easter Eggs & Secret Encounters
- **The Southern Sanctuary**: Find **Sam Altman** searching for trillion-dollar funding near an unstable Nuclear Plant.
- **The Quantum UFO**: Get abducted into an observation deck where **Albert Einstein** and a crying 80s-anime **Mark Zuckerberg** debate the monetization of the universe.
- **The Hidden Bunker**: A secret location under the mountain where **Zelensky** delivers a devastating final plea, and **Death** itself appears to void all your hard-earned signatures.
- **The Bezos Cinematic**: Trigger a high-stakes combat sequence (Street Fighter II style) against a bodybuilder **Jeff Bezos**.

### 🤖 C.L.A.U.D.I.A. Assistant
Your central hub is a satirical 90s digital mascot—a parody of modern AI assistants—housed in a CRT terminal. She provides helpful (and highly cynical) guidance on your path to bureaucratic salvation.

## 🎨 Technical & Aesthetic Identity

- **16-bit SNES Style**: Every character and landmark is a meticulously generated 16-bit pixel art asset.
- **ML Pipeline**: Assets are processed through a custom Python pipeline using `rembg` (U2-Net) for pixel-perfect transparency and resolution normalization.
- **VHS/CRT Effects**: Custom shaders provide a nostalgic 80s/90s television feel, complete with scanlines, chromatic aberration, and "Breaking News" ticker bars.
- **Procedural World**: The overworld is generated with thematic buildings, dirt paths, and dense forests, ensuring a consistent yet chaotic layout.

## 🛠️ Performance & Installation

1. **Prerequisites**: Godot Engine 4.3 (Standard).
2. **Setup**:
   - Clone the repository.
   - Open `project.godot` in the engine.
   - Run the `main.tscn` scene.
3. **Web Support**: Optimized for GLES3, with a custom CI/CD pipeline for GitHub Pages deployment.
