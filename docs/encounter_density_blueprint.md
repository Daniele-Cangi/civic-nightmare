# Encounter Density Blueprint

This document keeps the current satirical voice of each figure intact while making the civic procedure around them heavier, more embodied, and more consequential.

## Shared Grammar
Each main encounter should be built from five layers:

1. `Entry ritual`
The player performs a short administrative action before speech begins.
This should take 5-15 seconds and teach the room's logic physically.

2. `Voice`
The recognizable public rhetoric arrives only after the room has already sorted the citizen.

3. `Choice frame`
Both choices are reasonable.
Neither choice is clean.
The player chooses how the stain enters the file, not whether it enters.

4. `Residue`
The interaction leaves paper behind:
- receipt
- routing note
- reservation
- surcharge
- compliance marker
- review code

5. `Aftermath`
The room returns the player to the world with a small emotional cost:
- narrower route
- delayed exit
- new paper stack
- changed house beat
- altered checkpoint language

## Character Blueprints

### Donald Trump
- Entry ritual: take a loyalty badge, wait under a spotlight mark, place the form on the lectern seal.
- Voice: triumphant ranking language, direct flattery tests, theatrical deal-making.
- Choice frame:
  - Praise him and leave with a file marked as alignment.
  - Refuse the ritual and leave with a file marked as insufficient enthusiasm.
- Residue:
  - `gold endorsement slip`
  - `visibility compliance note`
- Aftermath:
  - the exit path is overlit
  - later desks speak as if the file is political, not civic

### Elon Musk
- Entry ritual: enter the premium lane, tap the scanner, wait for the gate to decide whether you count as a fast mover.
- Voice: product-launch irony, disruption framing, false informality.
- Choice frame:
  - Accept the convenience pitch and become quietly reranked.
  - Reject it and lose time while being treated as obsolete.
- Residue:
  - `throughput enrollment receipt`
  - `legacy routing exception`
- Aftermath:
  - floor arrows prefer machine-readable movement
  - one future corridor treats you as already profiled

### Ursula von der Leyen
- Entry ritual: collect a packet bundle, carry it to intake, wait while approval language circulates without ownership.
- Voice: managerial calm, conditional reassurance, procedural necessity.
- Choice frame:
  - Accept the timeline and be swallowed by process.
  - Push for acceleration and receive a valid signature with an attached reservation.
- Residue:
  - `derogation rider`
  - `committee pending annotation`
- Aftermath:
  - extra paper appears at home
  - later validation language becomes softer and more threatening at the same time

### Vladimir Putin
- Entry ritual: cross an exposed floor, stop on a surveillance marker, wait through a silence long enough to feel like a test.
- Voice: stillness, menace, understatement, threat disguised as casual observation.
- Choice frame:
  - Play along with the intimidation and lose trust in procedure.
  - Deflect it with humor and leave with higher bodily stress.
- Residue:
  - `watchlist acknowledgment`
  - `face remembered notation`
- Aftermath:
  - the walk back feels too empty
  - one later checkpoint reacts before you say anything

### Christine Lagarde
- Entry ritual: take a number, approach a clean desk, discover the free service has acquired layered charges before the paper is touched.
- Voice: elegant inevitability, financial composure, cost as common sense.
- Choice frame:
  - Ask for the total and receive the full arithmetic of your reduction.
  - Contest the premise and receive the same reduction in a more humiliating form.
- Residue:
  - `adjustment invoice`
  - `emotional surcharge notice`
- Aftermath:
  - new envelope at home
  - domestic space contracts a little more

### Emmanuel Macron
- Entry ritual: sit in the wrong chair, get repositioned into the "reflective" seat, wait through a tiny ceremonial pause before anything useful happens.
- Voice: abstraction, intellect, elegance, discursiveness, self-serious interpretation.
- Choice frame:
  - Listen and lose time while becoming complicit in the performance.
  - interrupt and obtain the signature while being marked as procedurally impatient.
- Residue:
  - `discursive delay transcript`
  - `haste notation`
- Aftermath:
  - the final walk feels longer because the room has made language itself heavy
  - the closing checkpoint treats reflection as another form of paperwork

## Implementation Rule
When this blueprint reaches code:
- `entry ritual` should become a short playable state
- `residue` should become a real document or flag in the file
- `aftermath` should hit space, house, or checkpoint language within one scene transition

The tone stays witty.
The system becomes heavier.
